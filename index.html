ABOUT ME

My name is Arturs Lapiņš, and for as long as I can remember I have been fascinated by technology—especially computers, their structure, and the endless possibilities of what can be achieved with them.

When I was 14, I received my very first computer, and that moment marked the beginning of my journey into developing practical skills in the field.

Over time, I designed logos for individual clients and began exploring 3D modeling with Blender, experimenting with how to create models and bring ideas to life in a virtual environment.

I am currently studying Computer Systems at Riga Technical University's Liepāja branch, where I continue to deepen my knowledge in programming and system design.
